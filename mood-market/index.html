Volatility means prices are moving a lot. It does not automatically mean something is good or bad. It means uncertainty is higher.

Bitcoin is often treated as a risk asset. When investors feel confident, crypto can rise. When they feel nervous, it can fall quickly.

A 24-hour change shows how much the price moved compared with yesterday. It helps you see short-term momentum.
